LoudProof - the solution to pleasant travel for individuals with ASD and auditory hypersensitivity

Find out how

Choose the sound you want to hear

There are many different environments that are challenging for those with ASD and audio hypersensitivity and wish to travel. With LoudProof, you can travel at ease with sound filtering for every situation. Begin by pairing the device with the app.

Quickly change the preset on the home screen by tapping the current mode.

Tap on Edit Sound Modes to adjust the settings for current presets, or create a new preset.

Adjust the volume cap for the selected preset until you reach a comfortable level.

The LoudProof device lets you know when someone is trying to communicate with you by using keyword recognition. Keywords can be toggled on and off to provide flexible use.

Features

Improving your travel experience

Find a quiet place

The LoudProof sound map allows users to find quiet locations nearby if necessary.

Interact with the device

Users can select the next preset in their list and adjust the volume directly on the device.

Keyword alerts

When the device detects a keyword, a directional signal tells you where the sound came from.

Full noise cancellation

LoudProof uses noise cancelling technology to block all sound when the user presses the mute button on the device, or selects it from the app.
